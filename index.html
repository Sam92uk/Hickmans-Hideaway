Welcome to Hickman's Hideaway!!

A hidden gem nestled in the heart of Tywyn. Step into a world of culinary enchantment as we invite you to embark on a remarkable dining experience like no other.

At Hickman's Hideaway, we are passionate about creating moments of delight through our exceptional cuisine and warm hospitality. Our talented chefs craft a menu that blends classic flavors with innovative twists, utilizing only the finest, locally sourced ingredients. Each dish is a work of art, meticulously prepared to captivate your taste buds and leave you craving for more.

Immerse yourself in the inviting ambiance of our restaurant, where rustic charm and contemporary elegance coexist harmoniously. Whether you're seeking an intimate dinner for two, a gathering with loved ones, or a memorable celebration, our attentive staff is dedicated to ensuring your visit is nothing short of extraordinary.

From our signature surf and turf, a tantalizing combination of succulent filet mignon and buttery lobster tail, to our carefully curated vegetarian options and mouthwatering desserts, every bite at Hickman's Hideaway is a journey of flavors and textures designed to ignite your senses.

Escape the ordinary and discover a hideaway where exquisite cuisine meets warm hospitality. Join us at Hickman's Hideaway and allow us to transport you to a world of culinary bliss that will linger in your memories long after the last bite.
